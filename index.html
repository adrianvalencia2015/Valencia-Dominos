📋 HOW TO PLAY: ALL FIVES ▼

OBJECTIVE

Play in teams. Score points by making open ends of the layout add up to a multiple of 5. First team to reach the goal wins.

PLAYERS & SETUP

  • 4 to 8 players, playing in teams. Partners sit across from each other.
  • Standard double-six set (28 dominoes). Draw hands, keep hidden.
  • Player left of the shuffler goes first. Play clockwise.

SCORING DURING PLAY

After each play, if open ends total a multiple of 5, score points: 5=1pt · 10=2pts · 15=3pts · 20=4pts · 25=5pts, and so on.

THE SPINNER & DOUBLES

  • The first double played is the spinner — layout can branch off it in multiple directions.
  • Later doubles are not new spinners; the line continues straight through them.
  • Exposed ends of all doubles count toward the open-end total.

END OF ROUND: WENT OUT

Count all remaining pips in opponents' hands. Round to nearest 5, divide by 5. Award those bonus points to the winning team.

Example: 27 pips → round to 25 → 5 bonus pts.

END OF ROUND: BLOCKED

Team with the fewest pips wins. Count all other teams' pips, round to nearest 5, divide by 5 → bonus points to winning team.
