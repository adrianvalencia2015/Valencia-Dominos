📋 HOW TO PLAY: ALL FIVES ▼

OBJECTIVE

Play in teams. Score points by making the open ends of the layout add up to a multiple of 5. First team to 250 points wins.

PLAYERS & SETUP

  • 4 to 8 players, playing in teams.
  • Partners sit across from each other when possible.
  • Uses a standard double-six set (28 dominoes).
  • Shuffle all dominoes face down. Each player draws their hand. Keep tiles hidden from the other team.

WHO GOES FIRST

The player to the left of whoever mixed/shuffled the dominoes goes first. Play moves clockwise from there.

STARTING THE ROUND

The first player may lead any domino. It does not have to be a double.

ON YOUR TURN

  • Play one domino that matches an open end.
  • If you cannot play, you pass.

SCORING DURING PLAY

After each play, add up all open ends. If the total is a multiple of 5, score points:

  • 5 = 1 point
  • 10 = 2 points
  • 15 = 3 points
  • 20 = 4 points
  • 25 = 5 points

THE SPINNER & DOUBLES

  • The first double played becomes the spinner.
  • The spinner may create multiple playable ends. The layout can branch off it in multiple directions.
  • Any double played after the spinner is placed sideways.
  • Later doubles do not become new spinners.
  • You do not branch off later doubles.
  • The line continues straight through them.
  • Their exposed ends still count toward scoring when totaling the board.

ENDING A ROUND

A round ends when either:

  • A player plays their last domino (goes out), or
  • The board is blocked and nobody can play

END OF ROUND: SOMEONE WENT OUT

  • Count all remaining pips in all other players' hands.
  • Add them together and round to the nearest 5.
  • Divide by 5. Those are the bonus points awarded to the winning team.

Example: 27 leftover pips → round to 25 → 5 bonus points.

END OF ROUND: BOARD BLOCKED

  • The team with the fewest pips remaining in hand wins the round.
  • Count all pips from all other teams' hands, round to nearest 5, divide by 5.
  • Those bonus points go to the team with the fewest pips.

WINNING

Keep a running total by team across all rounds. First team to 250 points wins the game.
